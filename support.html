Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a savings goal?

Tap the "+" button on the home screen, give your goal a name, set your target amount, choose a deadline, and pick how often you'd like to save. DreamJar will calculate your recommended deposit amount.

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. All your data is stored locally on your device. DreamJar never connects to your bank accounts or collects sensitive financial information. If you use iCloud sync (Plus feature), your data is encrypted in your personal iCloud account.

What's included in DreamJar Plus?

DreamJar Plus unlocks unlimited savings goals, smart reminders to keep you on track, iCloud sync across devices, and dark mode. Free users can manage one active goal.

How do I restore my subscription?

Go to Settings in the app and tap on the premium section. You'll find an option to restore purchases. Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.

Can I export my data?

Yes! Go to Settings > Export Data to download a copy of all your goals and deposit history.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. Go to Settings on your iPhone > Apple ID > Subscriptions > DreamJar to manage or cancel your subscription.
